World Religions and Their Beliefs

Hinduism

Hinduism believes in Dharma (duty), Karma (action), Samsara (rebirth), and Moksha (liberation).

Islam

Islam believes in one God (Allah), the teachings of Prophet Muhammad, and the Five Pillars of Islam.

Christianity

Christianity follows the teachings of Jesus Christ, focusing on love, forgiveness, and salvation.

Buddhism

Buddhism teaches the Four Noble Truths and the Eightfold Path to achieve Nirvana and end suffering.

Sikhism

Sikhism believes in one God, equality of all humans, selfless service, and truthful living.
