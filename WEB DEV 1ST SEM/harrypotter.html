Harry Potter – All 8 Parts

A magical journey of friendship, courage, and destiny

Part No. Movie Title Year Story / Context
1 Philosopher’s Stone 2001 Harry discovers he is a wizard and begins his journey at Hogwarts, learning about friendship and the dark past of Voldemort.
2 Chamber of Secrets 2002 A mysterious chamber is opened at Hogwarts, endangering students. Harry uncovers Tom Riddle’s secret.
3 Prisoner of Azkaban 2004 Harry learns the truth about Sirius Black and his parents, discovering loyalty and sacrifice.
4 Goblet of Fire 2005 Harry competes in the dangerous Triwizard Tournament, where Voldemort makes his terrifying return.
5 Order of the Phoenix 2007 Harry forms Dumbledore’s Army and fights against the Ministry’s denial of Voldemort’s return.
6 Half-Blood Prince 2009 Harry learns about Voldemort’s Horcruxes while darkness spreads and Hogwarts faces tragedy.
7 Deathly Hallows – Part 1 2010 Harry, Ron, and Hermione leave Hogwarts to destroy Horcruxes while being hunted by dark forces.
8 Deathly Hallows – Part 2 2011 The final battle at Hogwarts takes place, ending with the defeat of Voldemort and peace restored.
